Our Heritage

A legacy of automated excellence

Founded in 2010, Shree Varnika began with a singular vision: to revolutionise the traditional art of Vark (leaf) manufacturing. Where others relied on manual, often unhygienic processes, we saw an opportunity for purity through technology.

Under the guidance of Mr. Tara Shankar Singh, we pioneered the use of KV Automation technology, eliminating human touch from the critical stages of production. Every sheet of gold and silver that leaves our facility is not just luxurious, but certified hygienic and 100% vegetarian.
